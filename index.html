Vice Chacellor's DESK

It is a matter of immense pleasure for me to be a part of the Gurukulam Global Uniersity(Deemed to be University) family as its Vice Chancellor. I take this opportunity of welcoming students coming from all parts of the world, joining the university in undergraduate as well as postgraduate programmes. One needs to feel that they are just moving away from one family fold to another. In terms of credentials, the university has a long history of achievements. The most recent being the review by the University Grants Commission (2017), accreditation of the university by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) with highest A+ Grade (2017) and ranking of the university among the best universities of the country by the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) (2017). The university has highly qualified faculty in their respective fields of expertise to whom students can look up for any guidance, support and help to move ahead in life as professionals in the study programmes they have opted. A large number of national and international conferences, seminars and workshops are organized by the university that provide a variety of experiences to the students and inculcate skills such as leadership and team spirit which are helpful in their career. The learning experiences in the campus pave a strong path for enhancement of overall development of the learners.

Courses We Offer

Your Future Starts Here

Graduation

Graduation is a Four year course which is the basic of your career planning. This is the time when you start studying in the direction of your professionand have a clear understanding of what you have to do in your future and choose the course wisely.

Diploma

Diploma is a two year course which is more skill-based and career-oriented. Students basically choose a diploma course to gain in-depth knowledge and skills in a particular field wherein they want to build their careers, such as language, Management, etc.

Post Graduation

Post graduation is a master degree which is done after graduation. It is ideal for those who want to study a particular subject in greater detail, and it can help improve your employment opportunity. It includes M.A., MCA, MBA, M.Com, etc.
